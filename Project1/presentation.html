My Project

Hybrid E-commerce and Community Engagement Website with a focus on promoting sustainable living and eco-friendly products.

Inspired by:

Our Local Government Unit (LGU) project, we have been focusing on effective waste management practices, specifically the segregation of recyclable materials and non-biodegradable waste. Our project involves the collection and sorting of these materials, which are then directed to Material Recovery Facilities (MRFs). These facilities aim to efficiently process the materials and distribute them to small-scale manufacturers. These manufacturers, in turn, use the materials to create marketable products.

Identified Problem

  • The products manufactured from these materials have limited exposure.
  • The current approach faces limitations.
  • The production process is slow due to various factors.
  • Material Recovery Facilities have reached their maximum capacity

Our Goal

Facilitating the sustainable use of recycled waste materials to promote environmental responsibility.

Connecting sellers, manufacturers, and individuals dealing with 100% recycled waste materials.

How It Works

  • Sellers list their recycled materials.
  • Manufacturers browse and purchase materials for production.
  • Individuals can find unique recycled products.

Benefits

Discover the Advantages of RecraftLink:

  • Empowers individuals to find unique recycled products.
  • Connects businesses with a sustainable source of materials.
  • Promotes environmental responsibility and conscious consumption.
  • Fosters collaboration within the recycling and upcycling community.
  • Creates opportunities for innovation and creative reuse.
